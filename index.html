    How to read this

    One rupee is split into 100 paise

    The official budget infographic expresses expenditure as a rupee broken into paise. This app applies the same percentages to your number so the result stays intuitive.

    Important caveat

    Illustrative, not a personalized treasury ledger

    Taxes are pooled. Your exact rupees are not individually tagged to a ministry. This is a public-finance explainer built from official aggregate budget shares.
